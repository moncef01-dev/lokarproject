# Lokar - Digital Car Rental Marketplace
> A hybrid car rental marketplace combining digital booking with legally compliant offline validation.
Lokar is a professional digital car rental marketplace designed specifically for the Algerian market. It serves as a centralized hub connecting users with verified car rental agencies through a modern, trust-driven digital experience.

## 🌟 Product Overview

Lokar modernizes the car rental ecosystem in Algeria by bridging the gap between traditional agency workflows and digital efficiency. 

*   **Verified Marketplace**: A trusted platform where customers can browse fleets from authorized agencies.
*   **Hybrid Model**: A strategic approach that combines digital booking efficiency with the legal security of physical confirmation.
*   **Localized for Algeria**: Designed to handle regional challenges, including specific payment preferences and legal requirements.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Advanced Marketplace**: Powerful search and filtering across multiple agencies and vehicle categories.
*   **3-Step booking Flow**: A streamlined, high-conversion reservation process.
*   **Hybrid Validation**: Digital reservations followed by physical contract signing for maximum legal security.
*   **Simulated Payment System**: Prepared for **SATIM (CIB / Edahabia)** integration with support for "Pay at Pickup" options.
*   **Agency Management Suite**: Dedicated dashboards for fleet management, booking tracking, and performance analytics.
*   **Automated Contract Generation**: Instant, ready-to-print legal contracts generated directly from booking data.
*   **Secure Infrastructure**: Professional-grade authentication and session management.

---

## 📄 Automated Contract Generation

Lokar eliminates manual paperwork by automatically generating standardized, ready-to-print rental contracts.

*   **Data-Driven**: Contracts use real-time booking data, reducing errors and saving time.
*   **Standardized Compliance**: Ensures every rental agreement meets professional and legal standards.
*   **Finalized at Pickup**: The platform prepares the document; final legal validity is established at the agency through:
    *   Customer & Agency Signatures
    *   Official Agency Stamp

**Benefits:** Saves hours of manual work, reduces legal risk, and provides a professional appearance for agencies.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

Lokar is built using a modern, scalable full-stack architecture:

*   **Frontend**: React (Vite) & Tailwind CSS
*   **Backend**: Node.js & Express.js
*   **Database**: MongoDB (Mongoose ODM)

---

## 🔄 Booking & Payment Flow

1.  **Select**: User browses the marketplace and selects a filtered vehicle.
2.  **Reserve**: User completes the **3-Step Booking Flow** (Dates → Info → Payment).
3.  **Choose**: User selects between simulated card payment or "Pay at Pickup."
4.  **Confirm**: Booking is submitted and encrypted in the database.
5.  **Generate**: A PDF contract is automatically generated by the platform.
6.  **Print**: The agency receives the booking and prints the ready-made contract.
7.  **Sign**: Final agreement is signed and stamped during vehicle pickup.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Payment Simulation**: The current payment system is a high-fidelity simulation. The platform is architecturally prepared for **SATIM** integration but does not process real transactions yet.
