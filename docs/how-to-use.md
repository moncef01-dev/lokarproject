# Lokar: How-to-Use Guide

This guide explains how to get the most out of the Lokar marketplace, whether you are a customer looking for a car or an agency managing a professional fleet.

## 🧭 Quick User Flow

1.  **Search**: Use the home page to find the perfect vehicle.
2.  **Filter**: Refine your search by brand, category, or features.
3.  **Select**: Click on a vehicle to view its full specs and agency details.
4.  **Reserve**: Hit the "Reserve" button to start the booking process.
5.  **Book**: Complete the **3-Step Booking Flow**.
6.  **Confirm**: Choose your payment method and submit your reservation.

---

## ⚡ 3-Step Booking Flow

We've simplified the booking process into three logical steps designed for convenience and speed.

*   **Step 1: Rental Details**: Select your pickup and return dates and locations.
*   **Step 2: Personal Info**: Provide your name, contact details, and driver’s license information.
*   **Step 3: Payment Method**: Select either "Pay at Pickup" or simulated online card payment (**CIB / Edahabia**).

---

## 📑 The Contract Process

Lokar bridges the gap between digital booking and legal requirements through automated contract generation.

1.  **Automatic Generation**: Once your booking is confirmed, the system instantly creates a professional PDF rental agreement.
2.  **Agency Review**: The agency receives your booking and downloads the ready-made contract.
3.  **Physical Finalization**: During vehicle pickup, you and the agency will sign the **printed contract**.
4.  **Official Stamp**: The agency will add their official stamp to finalize the agreement.

**Note**: This hybrid process ensures that your rental is always legally secured and professional.

---

## 💰 Payment Information

*   **Simulation**: Currently, the online payment system (CIB/Edahabia) is a **high-fidelity simulation**. It is designed to prepare the platform for future SATIM integration.
*   **No Real Transactions**: No actual money is processed online at this stage. You can use the "Pay at Pickup" option for standard rentals.

---

## 🏢 Agency Workflow

If you are an agency user, your workflow is optimized for speed:

*   **Manage Fleet**: Add or update vehicles with high-quality images and detailed specs.
*   **Receive Bookings**: Monitor incoming requests from your dashboard.
*   **Download Contracts**: Click "Generate PDF" on any new booking to get the ready-to-print agreement.
*   **Finalize**: Print the agreement, have the customer sign it at pickup, and add your stamp.

---

## 🛡️ Admin Workflow

The platform administrators ensure the ecosystem remains secure and reliable:

*   **Agency Verification**: Review and approve new agency partners.
*   **System Monitoring**: Monitor marketplace activity to ensure high service standards.
*   **Audit Logs**: Access system logs to maintain platform integrity.
